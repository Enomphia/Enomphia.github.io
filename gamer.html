Triple H FM, Hornsby (triplehfm.com.au)

Volunteer Co-Host @ Generation HOPE / October - 2019

​Triple H 100.1FM is the Hornsby Ku-ring-gai Community Radio Station. Their programs connect with the interests of the vibrant and diverse Hornsby Ku-ring-gai Community. ​They give a voice to their community and broadcast an eclectic range of music and programs for all ages of their multicultural community. The program is being presented by Ms Jinky Marsh (www.jinkymarsh.com) who herself was nurtured at a young age. Ms Marsh is joined in by a local Youth co-presenter each month. This allows selected co-presenters to gain invaluable experience on radio broadcasting and presenting. It is an honour to be the first youth co-host for this program and it has helped me gain knowledge in public speaking and radio presenting.

Volunteer on NSW Cancer Council's Hornsby Relay for Life Organising Team

General Team Member / 2017 - 2019

Relay For Life is a community fundraising challenge for Cancer Council NSW. Teams of friends, families, schools and colleagues raise money in the lead up to an overnight event, where team members take turns walking around a track to signify that cancer never rests, so neither do we. Organised by passionate, local volunteers, each Relay event is an experience that will stay with you forever. We host activities designed to celebrate Survivors and recognise their Carers. We honour those we’ve lost during our candlelight ceremony, and we continue to raise funds to support people affected by cancer, fund research and improve cancer prevention.
